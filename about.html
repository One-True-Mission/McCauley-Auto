Who We Are

Same Trusted Service, New Name

What started as McCauley's Mobile Mechanic has grown into McCauley Automotive. Same trusted service, same commitment to honesty and quality, just a new name as we continue to grow.

We believe our job is about more than fixing vehicles. It is about taking care of the people who depend on them every single day. Vehicle repairs can be stressful, expensive, and overwhelming, so we work hard to keep our customers informed throughout the entire process and help find the best possible solution for their situation, not just the quickest one.

Our goal is simple: build trust, serve our community, and keep families safely on the road.
